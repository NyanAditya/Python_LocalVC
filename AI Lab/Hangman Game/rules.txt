HANGMAN GAME RULES

OBJECTIVE:
Guess the hidden word by suggesting letters one at a time before the drawing of a hangman is completed.

SETUP:
1. One player (or computer) thinks of a word and draws blank spaces representing each letter
2. A gallows is drawn with an empty noose
3. The guesser has 6 wrong guesses before losing

GAMEPLAY:
1. The guesser suggests a letter
2. If the letter appears in the word:
    - All instances of that letter are revealed in their correct positions
    - The guesser continues with another guess
3. If the letter does not appear in the word:
    - A body part is added to the hangman drawing
    - The incorrect letter is recorded as already guessed
4. The guesser cannot repeat previously guessed letters

HANGMAN DRAWING PROGRESSION (6 wrong guesses):
1. Head
2. Body
3. Left arm
4. Right arm
5. Left leg
6. Right leg

WINNING CONDITIONS:
- GUESSER WINS: All letters in the word are revealed before the drawing is complete
- GUESSER LOSES: The hangman drawing is completed (6 wrong guesses) before the word is guessed

ADDITIONAL RULES:
- Only letters A-Z are allowed (no numbers or special characters)
- Words should be common nouns, avoiding proper names or abbreviations
- The word length should be displayed to the guesser
- Case does not matter (A = a)
